Features

Everything projected.
Nothing in the way.

Dealer Button

Projected marker that rotates automatically or manually. Nothing physical to lose or argue about.

Blind Levels

SB, BB, and ante amounts displayed center-table where every player can see them at a glance.

Blind Timer

Countdown projected right on the felt. No more "how much time left?" — everyone sees the clock.

Seat Labels

Player positions clearly marked. Seat 1 through 10, so everyone knows where they sit in the action.

App Control

Set up your tournament structure, control timers, rotate dealer, adjust blinds — all from your phone.

Physical Remote

Optional wireless button — advance dealer, pause timer, start next hand — without touching your phone.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero hassle.

01

Mount

Clip TABLECAST overhead — ceiling, light fixture, or included arm. Point it at the table.

02

Configure

Open the app. Set player count, blind structure, timer rules, and display preferences.

03

Play

Hit start. The table lights up. Dealer rotates, blinds escalate, timers count — all on the felt.
